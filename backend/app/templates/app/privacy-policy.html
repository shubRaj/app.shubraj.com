Privacy Policy

Welcome to Shub Raj Apps. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your email address, first name, and last name when you personally sign up for our services. This information is used solely to provide a better user experience and will never be shared or sold to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, including ad networks, to display relevant advertisements on our website. These third-party providers may collect data such as your IP address and browser information for ad personalization. You can opt-out of personalized ads by managing your ad preferences with the respective providers.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to track and analyze user behavior on our website. This helps us improve our services by understanding how users interact with the site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data such as IP addresses, but you can opt-out by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, feel free to reach out to us at hello@shubraj.com.

Compliance with Privacy Regulations

California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA)

We comply with CalOPPA by ensuring users can visit our site anonymously. You will be notified of any privacy policy changes on this page, and you have the option to update your personal information by contacting us.

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. You can also request to restrict or object to the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@shubraj.com.

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) & California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA)

If you are a resident of California, you have the right to request information about the data we collect and how it's used. You also have the right to request the deletion of your personal information. To make such requests, please contact us at hello@shubraj.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please review this page periodically for updates.
