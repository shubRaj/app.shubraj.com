Why Use an EDV Photo Tool for Eligibility?

The EDV program has strict photo rules, and even small mistakes can lead to a disqualified application. A compliant photo shows your face clearly, with the right size, background, and lighting. Our EDV Photo Tool carefully checks for these details, ensuring your photo meets the guidelines and keeping you on track for a successful application.

What Our EDV Photo Tool Checks

Our EDV Photo Tool reviews your photo for these requirements:

  • Color: The photo must be in color, not black and white.
  • Size: The dimensions should be exactly 2x2 inches (51x51 mm) with a resolution of 600x600 pixels.
  • Recentness: It should be taken within the last six months to show your current appearance.
  • Background: A plain white or off-white background is needed for proper contrast.
  • Head Position: Your head should be centered, taking up about 50-70% of the photo.
  • Eye Position: Your eyes should be between 1 and 1 3/8 inches from the bottom of the photo.
  • Lighting: Lighting should be even with no shadows on your face or background.
  • No Accessories: Avoid glasses or any items that cover your face.

Tips for Taking a Great EDV Program Photo

To help you take a photo that’s already close to EDV standards, here are a few tips:

  • Stand against a plain white or light-colored wall to get a clean background.
  • Use lighting from both sides to avoid shadows on your face or background.
  • Don’t wear hats, glasses, or other items that cover your face, unless required for religious reasons.
  • Keep a neutral expression, looking straight at the camera with open eyes.
