Why Use a DV Photo Checker?

The DV program has specific photo requirements. A photo that doesn’t meet these standards may disqualify your application. Our DV Photo Checker reviews essential details like size, background, and lighting, helping you avoid common mistakes.

What the DV Photo Tool Checks

Our DV Photo Tool evaluates your photo based on these requirements:

  • Color: The photo must be in color, not black and white.
  • Size: 2x2 inches (51x51 mm) with a resolution of 600x600 pixels.
  • File Format: The photo must be in JPEG (.jpg) format.
  • File Size: The photo size must be equal to or less than 240 kB.
  • Aspect Ratio: The photo must be in a square aspect ratio, meaning the height and width must be equal.
  • Recency: Taken within the last six months.
  • Background: Plain white or off-white background.
  • Head Position: Centered, taking up about 50-70% of the photo.
  • Eye Position: Eyes should be 1 to 1 3/8 inches from the bottom.
  • Lighting: Even lighting, no shadows.
  • No Accessories: No glasses or items covering the face.

How Our DV Photo Tool Ensures Compliance

The DV Photo Tool not only checks but also adjusts minor photo issues to help meet DV requirements. Whether it’s background color or head positioning, our tool provides corrections so you can confidently submit a compliant photo.

Tips for Taking a Great DV Program Photo

Here are a few tips for taking a DV-compliant photo:

  • Use a plain white or light-colored wall as a background.
  • Ensure balanced lighting on your face to avoid shadows.
  • Remove hats, glasses, or other accessories unless required for religious purposes.
  • Keep a neutral expression, looking directly at the camera.
